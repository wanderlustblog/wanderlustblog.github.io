Our story

We Travel So You
Can Dream

WanderLust Blog was born from a one-way ticket and an insatiable need to see what lies beyond the horizon. Three years, six continents, and 120 countries later — we're still going. And we still feel wonder.

How it began

A Backpack, a Camera, and a Promise

In 2021, Maya and Luca quit their London office jobs, bought two backpacks, and booked a one-way flight to Lisbon. The plan: travel until the money ran out.

The money never quite ran out. Instead, they found a global community hungry for honest travel stories — not filtered, picture-perfect content, but the real ones. The missed buses. The unexpected kindnesses. The meals that changed everything.

WanderLust Blog became their way of sharing those stories — and inviting others to write their own chapters.

What we believe

Our Travel Philosophy

🌱

Slow & Sustainable

We believe in staying longer, spending locally, and leaving places better than we found them. No checkbox tourism. No bucket lists. Just presence.

🤝

Genuine Connection

The best travel moments happen at kitchen tables, on night trains, and in doorways — not at tourist attractions. We chase the human moments.

📖

Honest Storytelling

We don't do sponsored fluff. We tell you when a place disappointed us, when we got scammed, and when we cried in an airport bathroom.

🗺️

Go Off the Map

Our favourite places are the ones without Google reviews. We will always push you toward the road less taken — and tell you exactly how to find it.

💚

Respect First

Every place we visit has a community, an ecosystem, and a culture. We travel with humility and deep respect for the people whose home we're visiting.

✨

Wonder Always

No matter how many countries we visit, we try to see every new place with open eyes and open hearts — the way a child sees the sea for the first time.
