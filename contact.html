Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept guest posts? +
Yes! We love new voices. Send your story pitch and a brief bio to collab@wanderlustblog.com. WanderLust Blog looks for authentic, well-written travel stories with a unique perspective that our 85K readers will love.
Do you work with tourism boards or travel brands? +
We do, selectively. WanderLust Blog only partners with brands and destinations we genuinely believe in. All partnerships are clearly disclosed to our readers. Reach out with your proposal and we'll review it carefully.
Can I use WanderLust Blog's photos or content? +
Our content is copyright protected. For licensing enquiries, please contact hello@wanderlustblog.com. We're generally open to usage with proper credit and a small licensing fee.
How can I support WanderLust Blog? +
The best way is to read our stories, share them with fellow travellers, and subscribe to our newsletter. You can also support us on Ko-fi — buy us a coffee (or street snack, depending on the country).
Do you offer travel consulting or itinerary planning? +
Not officially, but the WanderLust Blog team loves helping fellow travellers. Drop us a message and we'll point you in the right direction based on our firsthand experience in 120+ countries.
